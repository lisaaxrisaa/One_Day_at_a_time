One Day at a Time

What is this for?

This web application is designed to help you efficiently manage your daily tasks. With it, you can easily add, edit, and delete tasks, as well as mark them as completed. Stay organized and on top of your to-do list with ease!

How To Use

Easily manage your tasks by adding, editing, deleting, and marking them as completed with just a few clicks.

Features

  • Add a Task: Click the “Add Task” button, enter the task details in the provided fields, and hit “Save” to add it to your list.
  • Edit a Task: Find the task you want to edit, click the “Edit” button next to it, make your changes, and then click “Save” to update it.
  • Delete a Task: To remove a task, click the “Delete” button next to the task you wish to remove. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  • Mark as Completed: When you finish a task, check the box next to it or click the “Mark as Completed” button to indicate it’s done.

Technologies Used

  • CSS
  • JavaScript
